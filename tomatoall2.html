Agridera Fresh Market Cucumber Hybrids

×××××

Agridera aims to be one of the world's leading companies in the breeding, production, and marketing of cucumber seeds. We invest intensively in research and development to provide solutions to growers and consumers in numerous countries around the world. In particular, we breed improved disease-resistant cultivars adapted to different markets globally.

Currently, emphasis is placed on combining resistance to Cucumber Mosaic Virus (CMV), Powdery Mildew (PM), and Downy Mildew (DM) with other soil-borne diseases into superior cucumber breeding lines and hybrids. Moreover, comprehensive efforts are being undertaken to develop varieties resistant to Cucumber Green Mottle Mosaic Virus (CGMMV).

In terms of horticultural traits, breeding is focused on earliness, different fruit sizes, crisp texture, deep green fruit color, flavor, crack resistance, shelf life, and high/low temperature fruit set. Combinations of quality features and disease resistance are sought in a variety of types, namely gherkins, long cucumbers, mid-sized cucumbers, and Asian varieties.

Agridera cucumber breeding programs are focusing on three main segments: Greenhouse Indeterminate Cucumbers, Open-field Indeterminate Cucumbers, and Bush Type Open-field Cucumbers.

Greenhouse Cucumbers Breeding Program

This breeding is aimed for the Spanish and Turkish markets. For single and cluster picks, with high resistances to CMV, PM, DM, CGMMV, and Nematodes. Some hybrids may include more resistances, such as Cladosporium and Leveillula Taurica.

Open-field Indeterminate Cucumbers

This breeding is for Brazilian, Moroccan, and Turkish markets. The main focus is on long and mid-sized fruits (18-25 cm), and resistances to CMV, PM, DM, and Nematodes. In the near future, Fusarium oxysporum (F3) will be included as basic resistance in our cultivars.

Open-field Bush Type Cucumbers

This breeding is for Mediterranean markets. We breed round and elongated shapes, standard sizes (10-15 cm), and mini cucumbers for pickling (6-9 cm). We combine resistances to CMV, PM, DM, and Nematodes as basic resistances in our hybrids. However, some hybrids may include more resistances, such as Cladosporium and Leveillula Taurica.

Agridera operates through international and local distributors around the world.
